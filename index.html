Facts About Blue Whales

Blue whales are the largest animals ever known to have lived on Earth. Here are some fascinating facts about them:

  • Size: Blue whales can grow up to 100 feet long and weigh as much as 200 tons.
  • Heart Size: The heart of a blue whale is the size of a small car.
  • Diet: Despite their massive size, blue whales primarily eat tiny shrimp-like animals called krill.
  • Sound: Blue whales are among the loudest animals on Earth; their calls can be heard over hundreds of miles underwater.
  • Lifespan: Blue whales can live for 70 to 90 years, with some living more than 100 years.
